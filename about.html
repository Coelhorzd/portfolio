I had dreamed about to become an INVENTOR since I was 8 or 9 years old. I wanted to IMPROVE AND CREATE things that people use everyday so THEIR LIVES would be BETTER (and maybe I would get rich on the process. why not?). But I ended up with a BSc in chemistry.


Chemistry experiment.


In my last year at college there was a serious fire accident in a night club in Brazil, I was not there but I kept wondering:


“ WHY DIDN'T SOMEONE CREATE A BETTER FIRE EXTINGUISHER YET? ”

“ HOW OLD IS CURRENT DESIGN? ”

I started from what makes a fire in order to understand how I could come up with a way to put an end to it. I found few ways but I picked the safest and less complicated one.

During those days I had to finish my studies so I put my messy notes/sketches aside for a while.


Fire extinguisher raw sketches.


At some point I found out that some guys in Indonesia had a similar idea couple years before and they already had a product called Elide fire ball so I moved on from that project.

Around mid 2019 I heard about UX design and started to study about it. Now here I am looking for an opportunity to help improving things.


Back to the question, WHO IS RENAN?

- A chemist, a problem solver and sometimes an inventor.
