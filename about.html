The story starts when I was 10 years old, since this age my dream was to become an INVENTOR. I wanted to IMPROVE AND CREATE things that people use everyday so THEIR LIVES would be BETTER (and maybe I would get rich on the process. why not?). But I could not move to another city to study mechanical engineering or industrial design so I got a BSc in chemistry.

When I was about to finish college there was a serious fire accident in a night club in Brazil and I kept wondering “ WHY DIDN'T SOMEONE CREATE A BETTER FIRE EXTINGUISHER YET? HOW OLD IS CURRENT DESIGN?” Then I had an idea (my own method for idea generation was similar to SCAMPER), I took some notes and kept doing my research while I also had to finish my studies.

At some point I found out that some guys in Indonesia have had a similar idea and they already had a product called Elide fire ball. So I saved my notes and moved on.

Some months ago I got to know about UX design and I got really interested on it because it impacts society as much as design of physical products. I have started to study about it and here I am looking for an opportunity to help changing the world.

WHO IS RENAN?

- A chemist who have always been a designer and sometimes inventor. Also a simple guy who enjoys photography (mostly landscape and street photography), playing basketball and table-tennis, watching sports, science fiction and documentaries, learning about other languages, culture and investiments. I also love traveling and reading about the lastests technologies.
