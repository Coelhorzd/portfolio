Since I was 10 years old I had dreamed about to become an INVENTOR. I wanted to IMPROVE AND CREATE things that people use everyday so THEIR LIVES would be BETTER (and maybe I would get rich on the process. why not?). But I ended up with a BSc in chemistry.

In my last year at college there was a serious fire accident in a night club in Brazil and I kept wondering:


“ WHY DIDN'T SOMEONE CREATE A BETTER FIRE EXTINGUISHER YET? HOW OLD IS CURRENT DESIGN?”

I kept thinking so I decided to approach the problem with a method. I started from what makes a fire in order to understand how I could put an end to it. So I found some ways but I picked the safest and less complicated one. But I had to finish my studies so I put my notes aside for a while.

At some point I found out that some guys in Indonesia have had a similar idea couple years before and they already had a product called Elide fire ball so I moved on.

Some months ago I heard about UX design and started to study about it. Now here I am looking for an opportunity to help change the world.


After all, WHO IS RENAN?


- A chemist who have always been a designer and sometimes an inventor. Despite that I'm also a simple guy who enjoys photography (mostly landscape and street photography), playing basketball and table-tennis, watching sports, science fiction and documentaries, learning about other languages, culture and investiments. I also love traveling and reading about the lastests technologies.
