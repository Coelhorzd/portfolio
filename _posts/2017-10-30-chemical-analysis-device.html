Context

As a chemist I have noticed how difficult is to get used to scientific research softwares. HPLC is one of the most used chemical analysis device around the world so I aim to find a way to make it more intuitive thus easier to learn and faster to use (specially for new users).

Design Thinking was the chosen approach.

Empathizing

User research

For the research I talked to a former professor I had when I studied at university and he introduced me to some of his master and doctorate degree students so I could interview him and those post-graduate students. I also observed them using the software while they were doig their research.
