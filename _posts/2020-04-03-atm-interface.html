Overview

The challenge

This UX design exercise was chosen after I observed people in a queue waiting to use an ATM machine.

Most used ATMs in Brazil are in the banks, but even that nowadays many people use their bank app we still have to use the ATM for some tasks. So it's a great opportunity for a project trying to improve ATM users experience.

This study case happens in Brazil so perhaps some points may only make sense for user experience at the ATM machines of brazilian banks.

Length of project

I did this project alone in my freetime and it took me around 3 months.

Process

Double diamond method was used on this project development.


Discovering

User research

On the first stage of research I interviewed some coworkers and relatives. After that a survey was created to be shared and test some assumptions, and also to understand better the current state.

There were 6 participants on this stage.

ATM in a brazilian bank



Defining

(pain points, problems)

The survey showed that to withdraw and make a deposit are the most often performed tasks on the ATMs. Yet, few users also transfer money, deal with loans details and get the validation to use bank app on their smartphones. But they would like to do everything on the app, if possible. Withdraw and make a deposit are impossible tasks to do on the app

Some users would like to be able to update personal information which on the app aswell which, for while, is done only if you go inside the bank to talk to someone.

But the most interesting point I have noticed is: To accomplish what you want when you go to the ATM, everyone still loses a lot of time. People lose time waiting on the line, people wait for the machine to recongnize their card, they wait for the machine to verify their password or biometrics, and they wait for the machine to deliver the money.
